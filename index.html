About Me

Hi, I’m Hanbee Choi, a Vancouver-based Motion Graphic Designer and UI/UX Designer with a flair for bringing screens to life! I bring two years of hands-on experience as a Motion Graphic Designer, where I’ve crafted engaging visuals that tell stories and captivate audiences. Blending the spark of motion with the finesse of user experience, I’m on a mission to create digital spaces that aren’t just pretty to look at but engaging to users.

My Story

During undergraduate years at Ewha Womans University, I chose media interaction design as my major as I was interested in design, especially motion graphic design. After graduation, I did a two internships and accumulated my work experience as a motion graphic designer. While working as a motion graphic designer, I created animations for the Government Innovation Fair website and had the opportunity to design a website at Sunshine Company. These experiences underscored the importance of interactive, user-centered design in enhancing digital experiences. This insight inspired me to pursue UI/UX design, leading me to Langara College to study web and mobile app design, where I could blend my visual design skills with a strong focus on usability and user experience, ultimately broadening my expertise to create cohesive and engaging interfaces.
